About the author

Anna Sutterer is a reader, writer, editor and overall word nerd. She's studied journalism at the University of Missouri, which introduced her to the fine art of storytelling for the public. She's reported at a daily newspaper, written for a weekly magazine, produced for a public radio program, marketed for a corporate company and worked at a literary magazine. Anna hopes to become a writer and editor who is worthy of entering and sharing diverse stories. She would like her and her readers to learn something about different people each day.
